Property Development & Brokerage
Investor Information Pack

CALDON™ is a private property development and investment company established in 2015, operating across New South Wales. Beyond our principal development activities, we provide comprehensive development brokerage services for qualified investors seeking exposure to the NSW property market without the complexity of managing the development process themselves.

Our model is simple: You provide the investment criteria and capital. We handle site sourcing, feasibility analysis, design, approvals, land acquisition, builder procurement, contract negotiation, construction management, and post-completion services. You own the asset — we deliver the project.

Our Process

01 Site Sourcing & Feasibility

We identify development opportunities matching your investment criteria across NSW. Each site undergoes rigorous feasibility analysis including market assessment, planning pathway review, preliminary design testing, and financial modelling.

You receive:
  • Qualified site options with option terms
  • Detailed feasibility study with financial projections
  • Risk assessment and planning pathway analysis
02 Design & Development Approval

Our team manages the complete design process, engaging architects and consultants, developing the optimal scheme, and navigating the DA process through council. We handle all consultant coordination, council negotiations, and approval conditions.

You receive:
  • Full architectural documentation
  • Development Approval (DA) consent
  • Conditions assessment and compliance pathway
03 Acquisition & Due Diligence

We negotiate purchase terms with vendors, coordinate legal due diligence, manage the contract process, and connect you to acquire the site. You purchase with DA approval in place and construction costs verified — significantly de-risking your investment.

You receive:
  • Negotiated purchase contract
  • Complete due diligence package
  • Settlement coordination
04 Tender & Contract Management

We prepare tender documentation, manage the builder selection process, evaluate submissions, and negotiate building contracts on your behalf. Our quantity surveying ensures accurate cost verification and appropriate risk allocation in contract terms.

You receive:
  • Competitive builder tender pricing
  • QS cost verification report
  • Negotiated building contract
05 Construction Management

Client-side project management throughout the construction phase. We monitor progress, review claims, manage variations, coordinate consultants, and protect your interests from site establishment through to practical completion and defects liability.

You receive:
  • Regular progress reporting
  • Claims and variation management
  • Practical completion and handover
06 Post-Construction Services

Upon completion, we provide a comprehensive quantity surveyor's depreciation schedule, maximising your tax benefits through capital works deductions and plant & equipment allowances. We also coordinate defect rectification during the liability period.

You receive:
  • Tax depreciation schedule (QS prepared)
  • Defects liability management
  • Final documentation package
