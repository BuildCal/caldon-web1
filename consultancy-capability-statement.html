About CALDON™ Consultancy

CALDON™ Consultancy is a leading provider of strategic quantity surveying, cost management, and project oversight services, serving clients across New South Wales—from bustling centres like Sydney and Newcastle to regional hubs such as Taree, Port Macquarie, and Tamworth.

With Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873 and membership in the Housing Industry Association (HIA), we offer independent, expert-led solutions that deliver value and certainty to commercial developers, residential builders, and property owners throughout NSW.
