Our Approach

Consultancy Philosophy

"Delivering precision, integrity, and expertise in every consultation."

At CALDON™ Consultancy, we provide independent, professional quantity surveying and cost planning services throughout New South Wales. Our consultancy practice offers expert guidance on budgeting, cost management, and project coordination for commercial and residential developments from Sydney to Newcastle, Port Macquarie, and beyond.

As registered practitioners (Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873) and proud members of the Housing Industry Association (HIA), we bring a wealth of experience and industry knowledge to every consultation. Our commitment to excellence and transparency ensures that clients receive accurate, actionable advice that supports informed decision-making.

Whether conducting development feasibility studies, providing expert testimony, or managing complex projects, we approach each consultation with the same dedication to detail, professional integrity, and client-focused service that has established our reputation across NSW.
