Consultancy Services

Quantity Surveying

Comprehensive quantity surveying services including detailed cost planning, bills of quantities, tender documentation, cost analysis, and financial certification throughout project lifecycles. Professional measurement and valuation services for all project phases.

Tender Management

Complete tender process management for builders and developers, including tender preparation, contractor evaluation, bid analysis, and selection recommendations. Streamlined procurement processes ensuring competitive pricing and qualified contractors.

Development Strategy & Feasibility

Strategic development advisory services including comprehensive feasibility studies, financial modelling, market analysis, risk assessment, and optimisation strategies. Expert guidance from concept through to project completion for maximum returns.

Cost Management

Site Acquisition Advisory

Expert site evaluation and acquisition advice leveraging our extensive development experience. Site analysis, due diligence, development potential assessment, and strategic acquisition guidance for optimal investment decisions.

Development Due Diligence

Comprehensive due diligence services for property acquisitions and development opportunities. Detailed analysis of planning constraints, development costs, market conditions, and risk factors to support informed investment decisions.

Our Approach

Consultancy Philosophy

"Delivering precision, expertise, and strategic insight from hands-on experience."

CALDON™ Consultancy leverages our extensive property development experience to provide exceptional advisory services across New South Wales. Our unique position as active developers gives our consultancy services unparalleled real-world insight and practical expertise.

As registered practitioners (Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873) with proven development experience, we understand the complexities of property development from both strategic and operational perspectives.
