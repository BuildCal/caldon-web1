Wattle Court Plants Roots in NSW Mid-Coast

My Australian News reports on the strategic partnership between CALDON™ and Wattle Court, highlighting the expansion into the Mid North Coast region with experienced local builder Brad Caldon.

In a milestone expansion for the boutique home-building network, Wattle Court has welcomed its newest franchise partner with the launch of Wattle Court Mid-Coast.

Formerly operating as Caldon and led by experienced builder Brad Caldon, the new office will service Taree, Port Macquarie, Wauchope, Forster, Gloucester and surrounding areas, combining deep local expertise with the nationally trusted Wattle Court framework to provide clients with a personalised, high-quality home-building experience.

Bringing more than two decades of construction experience, a multi-generational building legacy, and an award-winning reputation to the Wattle Court family, Brad sees the partnership as a dynamic step forward for the business.

"Joining Wattle Court means we can stay true to our roots in the community while also growing with new momentum," Brad said. "It's the best of both worlds, and we're excited to see where this journey takes us and our clients."

With more than 200 homes delivered and a track record for navigating complex builds on challenging rural and coastal blocks, Caldon's transition to Wattle Court is a move from strength to strength. Brad shared that he's pleased to be taking this next step with the team that has been by his side for years, along with their trusted network of suppliers and subcontractors.

"We're keeping the same crew and continuing the same quality service," Brad said. "It means our clients will still get the care and craftsmanship that they know us for, now with the fresh energy and vision of the Wattle Court brand."

Wattle Court CEO Matt Hope welcomed the new partnership, saying Brad's approach was a natural fit for the brand.

"Brad and his team bring together everything Wattle Court stands for: personalised craftsmanship, local knowledge, and a genuine care for the people they build for," Matt said. "We're thrilled to welcome them to the Wattle Court family and excited for what's ahead."

Looking ahead, Wattle Court Mid-Coast is already investing in the next phase of its evolution, with a brand-new display home currently in the early stages of development.
