Family Heritage

"Excellence in construction and craftsmanship is built through dedication, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to quality."

The CALDON™ family heritage represents nearly 70 years of dedicated involvement in the construction and property development industries across New South Wales. Our story is one of continuous learning, adaptation, and unwavering commitment to quality craftsmanship spanning from the late 1950s through today's modern business landscape.

From John Caldon's established leadership with Sorensen & Caldon Builders—contributing to over 1,000 homes built across the Manning Valley and earning five consecutive Master Builders Association Excellence Awards—through to Brad Caldon's innovative approach combining university-level construction management with fine craftsmanship and national media recognition, the family has contributed unique expertise while maintaining core values.

Today's CALDON™ represents the evolution of this accumulated knowledge and experience, bringing together traditional craftsmanship values, formal education, contemporary business acumen, and modern market insight gained through multiple successful ventures and industry recognition.

Regional Recognition
