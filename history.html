Family Heritage

"Excellence in construction and craftsmanship is built through dedication, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to quality."

The CALDON™ family heritage represents nearly 70 years of dedicated involvement in the construction and property development industries across New South Wales. Our story is one of continuous learning, adaptation, and unwavering commitment to quality craftsmanship spanning from the late 1950s through today's modern business landscape.

From John Caldon's established leadership with Sorensen & Caldon Builders—contributing to over 1,000 homes built across the Manning Valley and earning five consecutive Master Builders Association Excellence Awards—through to Brad Caldon's innovative approach combining university-level construction management with fine craftsmanship and national media recognition, the family has contributed unique expertise while maintaining core values.

Today's CALDON™ represents the evolution of this accumulated knowledge and experience, bringing together traditional craftsmanship values, formal education, contemporary business acumen, and modern market insight gained through multiple successful ventures and industry recognition.

Our Journey

Family Timeline

1950s

Caldon Family Beginnings

The Caldon family's construction legacy began in the 1950s, laying the groundwork for quality craftsmanship and community contribution in New South Wales.

1988

Sorensen & Caldon Builders Founded

John Caldon co-founds Sorensen & Caldon Builders in Taree, pioneering custom home building in the Manning Valley for over three decades, earning a reputation for tackling challenging projects and delivering exceptional quality.

1990s

Regional Recognition

Sorensen & Caldon Builders expands across the Mid North Coast from Taree to Port Macquarie, Coffs Harbour, and Newcastle, earning multiple HIA and MBA awards for their innovative designs and challenging builds.

2001

HIH Insurance Crisis Response

John Caldon contributes to industry recovery by completing unfinished homes on the South Coast of NSW, showcasing the family's commitment to client protection during the HIH crisis.

2003-2005

Brad's Education & Early Ventures

Brad Caldon begins his carpentry apprenticeship through TAFE and works with Sorensen & Caldon Builders while pursuing a Bachelor of Construction Management at the University of Newcastle.

2005-2010

Independent Business Development

Brad Caldon establishes his own ventures, applying his construction expertise and university education in independent enterprises like the Caldon Group, showcasing the family's entrepreneurial spirit.

2015

CALDON™ Established

Brad Caldon founds CALDON™, a dedicated property development and investment company, combining decades of family expertise with modern business practices across NSW.

2016-2018

Innovation in Craftsmanship

Brad Caldon and Tori Pitman launch HowsThatHouse in a two-car garage, specializing in handcrafted timber furniture and detailed joinery with coastal hardwoods.

2019

National Recognition

HowsThatHouse achieves breakthrough success through its partnership with The Block, earning praise from TV personalities Keith and Dan, leading to national media recognition.

2020-Present

Continued Excellence

CALDON™ continues to evolve, maintaining the family tradition of quality craftsmanship while embracing modern property development and investment strategies across New South Wales.
