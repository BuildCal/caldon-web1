Our Journey

Historical Timeline

1952

Foundation Years

The family business begins with a focus on quality craftsmanship and community service. Early ventures in construction and local development establish the foundation of trust and excellence that would define generations to come.

1970s

Expansion & Growth

Strategic expansion into commercial construction and property development. The second generation takes leadership, bringing new ideas while maintaining the core values of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction established by the founders.

1985

Innovation & Modernization

Introduction of modern construction techniques and business practices. Investment in new technologies and methodologies while preserving traditional craftsmanship values. Expansion of services to include property investment and development consulting.

1995

Regional Recognition

Establishment as a recognized name in NSW property development. Major projects completed across the Mid North Coast region. Awards for excellence in construction and community contribution. Formation of strategic partnerships with local businesses and institutions.

2000s

New Millennium Vision

Transition to third generation leadership brings fresh perspective on sustainable development and modern business practices. Focus shifts to strategic property investment alongside continued development activities. Digital transformation and modern marketing approaches adopted.

2015

CALDON™ Formation

Official establishment of CALDON™ as a dedicated property development and investment company. Consolidation of decades of experience under a focused business model. Strategic positioning for growth in selected NSW markets with emphasis on quality over quantity.

Present

Continued Excellence

Today, CALDON™ represents over seven decades of accumulated knowledge, relationships, and expertise in NSW property markets. We continue the family tradition of excellence while adapting to modern market conditions and opportunities.

Family Legacy

Generations of Excellence

I

The Founders

1952 - 1975

Building the Foundation

The first generation established the core principles that continue to guide our business today: uncompromising quality, honest dealing, and genuine care for community. Starting with small construction projects, they built a reputation for reliability and craftsmanship that opened doors to larger opportunities.

Their vision extended beyond immediate profits to long-term relationships and sustainable business practices that would benefit both clients and the broader community.

II

The Builders

1975 - 2000

Expansion & Innovation

The second generation took the solid foundation laid by their predecessors and built upon it with vision and ambition. They expanded into new markets, embraced emerging technologies, and developed the systematic approaches that would define modern property development practices.

This generation balanced respect for traditional values with openness to innovation, creating a business culture that honored the past while embracing the future.

III

The Strategists

2000 - Present

Modern Vision

Today's leadership combines decades of inherited wisdom with contemporary business acumen and market insight. They understand that success in modern property development requires both traditional values and cutting-edge strategies.

Under current leadership, CALDON™ has evolved into a sophisticated investment and development company while maintaining the personal touch and community focus that has always distinguished the family business.
