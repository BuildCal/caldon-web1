Our Foundation

Corporate Heritage

"Excellence in construction and property development is built through decades of dedication, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to quality."

CALDON™ represents the evolution of seven decades of construction excellence and property development expertise across New South Wales. Our corporate foundation is built upon a rich heritage of construction mastery, spanning from residential developments to complex commercial projects including hospital redevelopments and large-scale infrastructure.

From award-winning construction achievements—including over 1,000 homes delivered and five consecutive Master Builders Association Excellence Awards—through to modern property development and investment solutions, CALDON™ combines generational expertise with contemporary market insight and innovative business practices.

Today's CALDON™ is positioned as a premier property development and investment company, leveraging this accumulated knowledge and industry recognition to deliver exceptional results across residential, commercial, and investment sectors throughout New South Wales.

Our Journey

Legacy of Achievement

1950s

Foundation Era

Establishment of construction excellence and quality craftsmanship standards that would define decades of industry leadership across New South Wales.

1988

Corporate Establishment

Formation of Sorensen & Caldon Builders, pioneering custom construction solutions in the Manning Valley and establishing the foundation for decades of industry recognition.

1990s

Regional Expansion

Strategic expansion across Mid North Coast regions from Taree to Port Macquarie, Coffs Harbour, and Newcastle, earning multiple HIA and MBA awards for construction innovation.

2000s

Industry Leadership

Achievement of over 1,000 residential projects delivered and five consecutive Master Builders Association Excellence Awards, establishing market leadership in construction quality.

2010s

Diversification & Innovation

Expansion into complex commercial construction including hospital redevelopments and large-scale infrastructure projects, demonstrating capabilities across diverse construction sectors.

2015

CALDON™ Evolution

Strategic evolution into dedicated property development and investment, combining seven decades of construction expertise with modern development and investment strategies.

2019

National Recognition

Achievement of national media recognition and industry acclaim through exceptional craftsmanship, establishing CALDON™ as a premier development enterprise with uncompromising standards.

2021-Present

Market Leadership

Continued evolution as a leading property development and investment company, maintaining construction excellence while embracing modern market strategies across New South Wales.
