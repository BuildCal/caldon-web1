Family Heritage

"Excellence in construction and craftsmanship is built through dedication, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to quality."

The CALDON™ family heritage represents decades of dedicated involvement in the construction and property development industries across New South Wales. Our story is one of continuous learning, adaptation, and unwavering commitment to quality craftsmanship.

From John Caldon's established presence in Taree's construction industry through to Brad Caldon's innovative approach to fine joinery and property development, the family has contributed unique expertise while maintaining core values of precision, integrity, and genuine care for clients and community.

Today's CALDON™ represents the evolution of this accumulated knowledge and experience, bringing together traditional craftsmanship values with contemporary business acumen, formal education, and modern market insight.

Our Journey

Family Timeline

1982

Early Foundations

The beginning of what would become decades of family involvement in the NSW construction industry, establishing the foundation of expertise and reputation that continues to guide CALDON™ today.

1988

Taree Construction Leadership

John Caldon's involvement with Sorensen & Caldon Builders becomes established in Taree, beginning over three decades of building excellence in the Manning Valley region and surrounding areas.

1990s

Industry Recognition

The family business earns multiple Housing Industry Association (HIA) and Master Builders Association (MBA) awards, establishing a reputation for quality construction and challenging projects across the Mid North Coast.

Early 2000s

Next Generation Education

Brad Caldon begins his carpentry apprenticeship through TAFE while working in construction, but chooses to pursue higher education, completing the Open Foundation program and Bachelor of Construction Management at the University of Newcastle.

Mid-2000s

Independent Entrepreneurship

Brad Caldon establishes his own ventures, choosing to apply his construction expertise and university education in independent enterprises, demonstrating the family's entrepreneurial spirit and adaptability.

2015

CALDON™ Established

Brad Caldon establishes CALDON™ as a dedicated property development and investment company, bringing together decades of family expertise with modern business practices and strategic market positioning across NSW.

2016

HowsThatHouse Founded

Brad and his partner Tori found HowsThatHouse in a two-car garage at 13 Cornwall Street, Taree, specializing in handcrafted timber furniture and detailed joinery, showcasing the evolution of traditional craftsmanship into contemporary markets.

2019

National Recognition

HowsThatHouse achieves breakthrough success through The Block TV partnership, leading to collaboration with The Block Shop and generating 30% of business revenue, demonstrating how traditional craftsmanship can reach national markets.

2020

Industry Transition

John Caldon transitions from construction business ownership, marking the end of direct family involvement in Sorensen & Caldon Builders while both John and Brad pursue independent ventures in their respective fields of expertise.

2021

Community Leadership

John Caldon becomes president of Team Taree (formerly Taree Business Chamber), transitioning his decades of business expertise toward community leadership, economic development, and disaster relief coordination for the Manning Valley region.

Present

Continued Excellence

CALDON™ and HowsThatHouse represent the continuation of decades of accumulated knowledge, relationships, and expertise. The family tradition of excellence continues while adapting to modern market conditions and opportunities across multiple industries.

Our Evolution

The CALDON™ Journey

"True excellence emerges when traditional expertise meets modern innovation, creating new possibilities while honoring proven values."

The establishment of CALDON™ in 2015 represented the natural evolution of decades of family involvement in NSW construction and property development. Rather than simply continuing traditional paths, CALDON™ was founded upon a solid foundation of accumulated expertise, established relationships, and proven capabilities enhanced by formal education and contemporary business strategies.

This heritage provides CALDON™ with distinct advantages: deep understanding of quality construction practices, intimate knowledge of regional markets, the wisdom gained from multiple market cycles, and the innovation that comes from combining traditional expertise with modern education and fresh perspectives.

Our approach honors traditional values—quality, integrity, and genuine service—while embracing contemporary business practices and modern market opportunities. This blend of heritage and innovation, coupled with formal education and independent entrepreneurial experience, positions CALDON™ to deliver exceptional results in today's competitive property development landscape.
