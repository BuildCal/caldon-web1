What We Do

Residential Design & Construction

Class 2 Building Construction

Specialised construction of multi-residential buildings, including apartments and townhouses, adhering to stringent codes and delivering durable, high-quality outcomes as registered practitioners.

Our Philosophy

"Building trust through quality, expertise, and integrity."

At CALDON™, we are a dual-focused firm specialising in both construction and consultancy services across New South Wales. Our construction expertise delivers premium residential homes and sophisticated commercial buildings, with service extending from Port Macquarie to Buladelah, Taree, west to Gloucester, and east to Forster Tuncurry. Simultaneously, our consultancy offerings provide expert guidance on compliance, cost management, and project coordination for commercial and multi-residential developments throughout NSW.

As registered practitioners (Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873), licensed builders (Licence 377088C), and proud members of the Housing Industry Association (HIA), we ensure every project reflects our commitment to industry-leading standards, whether we're constructing your vision or providing strategic consultancy to optimise your development.
