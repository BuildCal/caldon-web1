Construction Capability Statement

Premium Residential and Commercial Construction Across NSW’s Mid North Coast

About CALDON™ Construction

CALDON™ Construction specialises in delivering premium residential and commercial construction services across the Mid North Coast of NSW, spanning from Port Macquarie to Buladelah, west to Gloucester, and east to Forster Tuncurry.

With Building Practitioner Number BUP0002873, Builders Licence 377088C, and membership in the Housing Industry Association (HIA), we ensure compliance with the *Building Code of Australia (BCA)*, *Design and Building Practitioners Act 2020 (DBPA)*, and NSW regulations, delivering projects with precision and quality.

Construction Services

Custom Home Design & Construction

We design and construct bespoke homes tailored to client specifications, adhering to the *Home Building Act 1989 (NSW)* and *BCA* Volume 2 requirements for Class 1 buildings.

  • Compliance with *AS 1684* for timber framing and *AS 4100* for steel structures
  • Integration of *AS 3959* for bushfire-prone areas, common in Mid North Coast
  • Use of locally sourced, *AS/NZS 2269*-compliant materials
  • Collaboration with architects registered under *DBPA* Part 2
  • Energy efficiency meeting *BCA* Section J and *BASIX* standards

Class 2 Multi-Residential

We deliver multi-residential buildings (e.g., apartments, townhouses) compliant with *BCA* Volume 1 for Class 2 structures and *DBPA* requirements for design declarations.

  • Fire safety systems per *AS 1670.1* and *BCA* Part E
  • Waterproofing to *AS 3740* and *BCA* Part F1
  • Structural design per *AS 3600* (concrete) and *AS 4100* (steel)
  • Accessibility compliance with *Disability (Access to Premises – Buildings) Standards 2010*
  • Lodgement of design and building declarations via NSW Planning Portal

Commercial Construction

We construct retail, office, and industrial spaces adhering to *BCA* Volume 1 (Classes 5-9) and NSW *Environmental Planning and Assessment Act 1979*.

  • Fire resistance per *BCA* Part C and *AS 1530*
  • Mechanical ventilation to *AS 1668.2* for indoor air quality
  • Load-bearing compliance with *AS 1170* for structural actions
  • Health and safety per *Work Health and Safety Act 2011 (NSW)*
  • Development approval coordination with local councils (e.g., MidCoast Council)

Residential Extensions

We provide extensions that integrate with existing Class 1 dwellings, compliant with *BCA* Volume 2 and *Home Building Act 1989* licensing requirements.

  • Structural continuity per *AS 2870* for residential slabs and footings
  • Weatherproofing to *AS 3500* plumbing standards
  • Thermal performance meeting *BCA* Part 3.12 and *BASIX*
  • Approval via Complying Development Certificate (CDC) or DA under *EPA Act*
  • Minimal disruption with SafeWork NSW-compliant site management

Building Refurbishment

We refurbish existing structures, ensuring compliance with *BCA* upgrades and *Heritage Act 1977 (NSW)* where applicable.

  • Fire safety retrofits per *BCA* Part E and *AS 1851*
  • Waterproofing upgrades to *AS 4654* for external elements
  • Electrical systems per *AS/NZS 3000* Wiring Rules
  • Heritage preservation under *NSW Heritage Council* guidelines
  • Building condition reports for *DBPA* compliance

Heritage Construction

We specialise in heritage builds, blending traditional methods with modern standards, compliant with *Heritage Act 1977* and *BCA* exemptions.

  • Use of *AS 3700* for masonry restoration
  • Timber repairs per *AS 4786* durability standards
  • Approval via *Heritage Council of NSW* for listed properties
  • Retention of historical features per *Burra Charter* principles
  • Modern upgrades (e.g., *AS 1428* accessibility) where permitted

Sustainable Building

We implement eco-friendly construction practices meeting *BCA* Section J, *BASIX*, and NSW sustainability policies.

  • Insulation to *AS 4859* for thermal efficiency
  • Solar passive design per *BCA* Part 3.12
  • Water efficiency per *AS/NZS 3500* and *BASIX* targets
  • Use of recycled materials under *Green Star* principles
  • Carbon footprint reduction aligned with *NSW Net Zero Plan*

Commercial Fit Outs

We deliver interior fit outs for commercial spaces, compliant with *BCA* Volume 1 and *Work Health and Safety Regulation 2017 (NSW)*.

  • Fire safety per *AS 1670.4* for detection systems
  • Lighting to *AS/NZS 1680* for workplace standards
  • Acoustic performance per *BCA* Part F5
  • Fit out approval via CDC or DA under *EPA Act*
  • Custom joinery and finishes per client specifications

Construction Process

1. Initial Consultation

Define vision, requirements, budget, and timeline, ensuring alignment with *Home Building Act* contract requirements.

2. Design & Planning

Create detailed plans compliant with *DBPA* design declarations and *BCA* standards.

3. Cost Estimation

Provide transparent estimates per *Australian Cost Management Manuals* and *Home Building Act* disclosure rules.

4. Approvals & Documentation

Secure permits via NSW Planning Portal, meeting *EPA Act* and local council requirements.

5. Construction

Execute build with *SafeWork NSW* safety compliance and *BCA* quality controls.

6. Project Completion & Handover

Inspect per *DBPA* building declarations, deliver with warranties under *Home Building Act*.
